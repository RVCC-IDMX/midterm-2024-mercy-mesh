ODDS & SOBS

A crochet community working in the loops. Our mission is to explore the limits of crochet together, this is a safe net made to post your projects, ideas or even your worst fails!. Here, you can share and download patterns, engage in lively discussions, and receive valuable feedback on your projects. Whether you're looking for resources to improve your skills, opportunities to collaborate, or just a place to connect with fellow enthusiasts, you've found your space.

PROJECTS

STITCH YOUR MARK!

Make an intervention in a public space and send us pictures of your process, result and a before and after of your work. The winners will receive up to $500 and a full article in our website. Submission deadline is June 5, 2024.

To get more information, email oddsandsobs@crochet.org we will be happy to answer all of your questions

SAD CLOWN: Crochet Patterns for the Melancholic

We are creating a collection of crochet patterns with the theme "Sad Clowns". This compilation aims to explore the emotions of these touching characters. The credits of each pattern will be included, and we will also open the option to "tip the artist".

This library is open and constantly expanding - Feel free to participate.

PLAYLIST ALERT!
